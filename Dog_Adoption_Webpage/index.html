Welcome to our website!

MuttsnMittens is a boarding facility for both dogs and cats. Established in 2003, we provide both short-term and long-term boarding. We were originally located at Pasir Ris farmway, where our compound was able to hold both our dogs and cats. Since the relocation of shelters, our animals have been shifted to 2 locations - our dogs at Sungei Tengah and our cats at Guillemard. We also provide volunteering opportunities for individuals and corporate groups.

A corporate partnership has allowed for us to build a new compound - MuttsnMittens Active!
This state-of-the-art facility features both boarding rooms for canines as well as a swimming pool where they can splash away! Stay tuned for the official opening of this facility! We are sure that your pooches will have a splashing great time here.

With over 100 dogs and 70 cats in our care, we also provide adoption services to suitable households. Take a look at the animals we have under our care and perhaps you'll be able to find your soulmate! If you are not able to adopt, fret not, there are also fostering and sponsorship opportunities. Whether you are here for adoption or boarding, we ensure that we will do our best to cater to your needs.
